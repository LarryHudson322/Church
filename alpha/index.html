About Me

Pastor Wallace Welch of The World’s Church of the Living God of Florida has been both an inspiration and example of God’s word to the people of St. Lucie County and parts of the world. He was called to ministry at the church and has been ministering, counseling and encouraging individuals to strive toward perfection in Jesus Christ. “For God created every man and woman giving them meaning and purpose with a clear vision,” said Welch. Welch describes his life as a tribute to the greatness of God. Through God, Welch has become a pastor, entrepreneur, engineer and author. At the age of 19, he felt the calling to do God’s work and took upon different works within the ministry — first holding as deacon, then as associate minister and finally as pastor.
